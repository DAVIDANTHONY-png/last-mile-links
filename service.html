Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our services.

How long does ICT lab setup typically take?

Most ICT lab installations are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on the size and complexity. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your operations.

What are the requirements for Academy enrollment?

Basic computer literacy is recommended but not required. You need a willingness to learn and either a personal laptop or the option to use our facilities. Payment plans are available.

How does agricultural financing work?

We partner with microfinance institutions to provide loans with flexible repayment terms. After assessing your farm and business plan, we connect you with appropriate funding options.

Do you offer support after project completion?

Absolutely. All our services include a support period: 6 months for ICT installations, lifetime alumni network for Academy graduates, and ongoing extension services for farmers.

Can I switch between service tracks?

Yes, many of our clients use multiple services. For example, Academy graduates often return for ICT consultation for their businesses, and farmers sometimes join our digital literacy programs.
